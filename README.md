# Customer Retention & Churn Analysis

## Project Overview

Customer churn is one of the most critical challenges faced by subscription-based businesses. This project focuses on analyzing customer behavior, identifying churn patterns, and discovering factors that influence customer retention.

Using customer subscription data, the analysis provides actionable insights that can help businesses improve customer satisfaction, increase retention rates, and reduce revenue loss caused by customer attrition.

---

## Problem Statement

Businesses often struggle to understand why customers leave their services. The objective of this project is to analyze customer data and identify the key factors contributing to customer churn while providing recommendations to improve customer retention.

---

## Objectives

* Analyze customer churn behavior
* Calculate churn and retention rates
* Identify high-risk customer segments
* Study customer lifetime patterns
* Evaluate the impact of contract types, payment methods, and services on churn
* Generate business recommendations to improve customer retention

# Key Insights

### Insight 1

Customers with month-to-month contracts exhibited the highest churn rates.

### Insight 2

Customers with shorter tenure were more likely to leave the service.

### Insight 3

Electronic check users showed relatively higher churn behavior compared to other payment methods.

### Insight 4

Long-term contracts significantly improved customer retention.

### Insight 5

Customers with higher monthly charges demonstrated increased churn tendencies.

---

## Business Recommendations

* Encourage customers to adopt long-term contracts through incentives and discounts.
* Improve onboarding experiences for new customers.
* Launch targeted retention campaigns for high-risk customer groups.
* Enhance customer support and service quality.
* Develop loyalty and reward programs for long-term customers.
